Web, Print Design and 3D Rendering Projects
Background
I was raised in the bustling street markets of Hong Kong, where double shelters and vivid canvases dangled from frames. The hustle and bustle of it all kindled my passion for art and design. My interest in graphic communication traces back to my secondary school days, when I served as a librarian and took charge of decorating the notice boards every month.
My Works
My work primarily centres around web and print media. I collaborated with the Art Department at the Education University and produced several printed promotional materials while teaching there. Additionally, I have worked as a freelance developer and designer, managing HTML and CSS coding for responsive web design and legacy Flash websites. Apart from print media, I have also assisted with documentary photography and 3D rendering on various projects.
